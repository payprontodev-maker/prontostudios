Last updated: January 2026

1. OVERVIEW

By placing an order with Pronto Studios, you agree to the following terms. Pronto Studios provides video editing, photo editing, and content creation services for the game Street Wars 2. All business is conducted through Discord, and payments are processed via PayPal.

💬 All communication regarding your order takes place on Discord. After payment, a team member will DM you to collect your details and begin work. So make sure to enter your real username.

2. ORDERING & PAYMENT

All payments are made in advance through PayPal before any work begins. Prices are listed in USD and are final at the time of purchase.

  • Payments are non-refundable once work has started (see Refund Policy below).
  • By paying, you confirm you have read and agree to these terms.
  • PayPal buyer protection may apply — we recommend familiarizing yourself with PayPal's policies.
  • We do not accept any other payment methods at this time.

3. DELIVERY

Turnaround time varies by service and will be communicated when we DM you on Discord. We aim to complete all orders within a reasonable timeframe, but complex projects may take longer. We will keep you updated throughout the process.

  • Delivery timelines begin once all required assets and details have been received from the client.
  • We are not responsible for delays caused by the client failing to provide materials.
  • Finished files are delivered via Discord DM or a file-sharing link.

4. REVISIONS

Each service includes at least one round of revisions. Revisions must be requested within 48 hours of delivery. Revision requests must be reasonable and within the scope of the original order.

  • Revisions do not include entirely new creative directions or additional services.
  • Additional revision rounds beyond what is included may be charged separately.

5. REFUND POLICY

We offer refunds in the following circumstances:

  • We are unable to complete your order within a reasonable time and you have not received any work.
  • The final delivery does not match the agreed-upon scope of work.

Refunds will not be issued in the following circumstances:

  • Work has been started or completed.
  • You simply change your mind after payment.
  • You failed to provide required assets or details within a reasonable timeframe.

⚠️ If you have a dispute, please reach out to us on Discord first. We will always try to resolve issues fairly before a PayPal dispute is opened.

  • Providing all required footage, assets, and instructions in a timely manner.
